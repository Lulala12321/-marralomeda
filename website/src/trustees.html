Trustees

Sister Kathleen Rushton, RSM (Chairperson)

Formerly a secondary school teacher, Sister Kathleen holds a PhD in the gospel of John in which she continues to research and write: works in adult education teaching Scripture; and in spiritual direction and formation.

Sally Thompson QSM, JP (Deputy Chair)

Sally was an early childhood teacher for 25 years and then a Christchurch City Councillor. She has a wide active interest in several community organisations including the Christchurch North Presbyterian Church.

Denise Holling- Dip Teaching (Tertiary)

Denise is the Family Representative on the Marralomeda Board. Her sister (Monica) is a member of the Marralomeda community. More recently retired, Denise worked for 35 years at the Polytechnic as a tutor and as an academic manager. She is an active member of St Vincent de Paul Conference at St Peter’s in Beckenham.

Ruth Jones, QSM, BSW (Hons)

Tū mai rā maunga Hikurangi

E rere atu rā ki te awa o Waiapu

Nei rā a Porou e mihi atu nei

Ruth was born in Christchurch, has lived experience of disability and remains close to her family and friends here. Ruth is proud to serve and work in the communities that she is part of. Initially as a social worker, then a manager and now as a consultant with Kanohi ki te Kanohi Consultancy, Ruth provides and encourages leadership in the community, NGO, education and disability sectors.

She and her husband Gary established the Earthquake Disability Leadership Group to promote the need for the new Christchurch to be a genuinely accessible city.

Matthew Martin, LLB (Hons)

Matthew is a lawyer who joined the Trust in January 2016. He is committed to the values underpinning Marralomeda, and considers the community members to be some of life’s greatest teachers.

Michael Schimanski - CA, BCM

Michael is a chartered accountant and a senior partner of Kendons, a chartered accounting practice in Christchurch. He is a Trustee and deputy Chairman of the Catholic Development Fund. He is also Secretary of the Christchurch Diocesan Foundation, a member of the St Joseph’s Papanui Parish Finance team and a Trustee on The Marralomeda Charitable Trust Board. Michael is married to Tania and they have two daughters.
